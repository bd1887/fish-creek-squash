Lessons

Our club boasts three of the finest squash instructors in all of Fish Creek at the affordable price of $35 per half-hour lesson (includes court reservation fee).

Coach Kiefer
Kiefer is an NCCP level 2 squash coach currently working towards his level 3 certification. Kiefer has been coaching for 6 years and has hundreds of hours experience with players of all levels, ages, and abilities.

Coach Leona
Leona is an NCCP Level 2 squash coach and has been an advocate for junior squash in Alberta and the prosperity of the sport. As a player, Leona played for Fish Creek on the junior national team from 1996-99 and was selected for world junior championships.

Coach Matt
Matt in an NCCP Level 3 squash coach who has previously worked as the Technical Director and Provincial Coach for Squash Alberta.
