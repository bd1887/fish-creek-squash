The Club

Conveniently located in the northeastern corner of southwest Fish Creek, our club boasts three squash courts, two racquetball courts, a full gym with cardio and weight training sections, a fully licensed bar, and change rooms with steam rooms and showers.

Memberships options at Fish Creek Sports Club are flexible and affordable. Guests and the public are welcome for a $15.00 drop in fee.
